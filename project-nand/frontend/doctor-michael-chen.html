About Dr. Michael Chen

Dr. Michael Chen is a distinguished Interventional Cardiologist at MedCare Hospital, specializing in minimally invasive cardiac procedures with over 12 years of clinical experience.

Known for his expertise in complex coronary interventions and structural heart procedures, Dr. Chen has performed over 1,000 successful cardiac catheterizations and interventional procedures. His approach combines cutting-edge techniques with compassionate patient care.
